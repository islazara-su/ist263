Dance Mission embodies the values of the city of San Francisco emphasizing inclusion, fairness, and justice. You are most likely to find Salsa, Hip Hop, Drumming/Dance, or fusion classes here. The atmosphere is upbeat and fun, with classes for both youth and adults

Beginner to Advanced/Professional classes in all styles but widely recognized for their ballet and modern/contemporary classes. While in San Francisco you can also watch their professional company perform. The studio atmosphere is professional, in their drop in classes you will mostly find adults but all ages are encouraged. They also offer workshops and a youth summer ballet intensive that is highly regarded

From beginner to advanced classes this studio is known for offering a lot of different dance techniques and styles. The studio atmosphere is comfortable with a welcoming open space and large dance studios, you will find a diverse age range of dancers here with differing skill levels. For beginners, they offer Rhythm and Motion classes to introduce dance concepts where the more advanced dancer can find a class offered by local artists in residence with their own dance companies.
